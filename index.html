Driver Mechanics: Slice Prevention

  • 🛑 The Issue: Over-the-Top Path (Outside-in slice).
  • 💡 The Fix: "Pump Drill" - Start downswing by bumping hips toward target, keeping back to target longer.
  • ⛳ Goal: Cut the big right-to-left slices in half over 20 balls.

7-Iron Fundamentals

  • 🌱 Tempo: "1-and-2" smooth backswing count.
  • 🌱 Impact: "Sweep the grass" - imagine brushing the turf after the ball.
  • 🌱 Power: "Bump-turn-swing" sequence. Finish balanced and tall!

Golf IQ Knowledge Base 🧠

Glossary and answers to the wider world of golf.

Tour Formats: PGA vs. LIV
  • 📺 Event Length: PGA Tour is usually 72 holes (4 days). LIV Golf is generally 54 holes (3 days, LIV = 54 in Roman numerals), though newer events like Hong Kong 2026 introduced 72-hole formats.
  • 📺 Field Size & Cuts: PGA has big fields (~150) and cuts half the players after day 2. LIV has smaller fields (~54) and no cuts.
  • 📺 Tee Times: PGA uses traditional 1st/10th tee starts. LIV uses shotgun starts (everyone starts at once on different holes).
  • 📺 Teams: LIV includes a team competition element alongside individual scoring.
Scoring & Handicaps (WHS)

A handicap (Handicap Index) levels the playing field, allowing beginners to compete fairly against pros.

  • 📉 Max Handicap: 54.0. Beginners usually start here or around 30+.
  • 📉 How it works: If you are a 30 handicap and shoot 102 (Gross), your net score is 72.
  • 📉 Tracking in NZ: Official handicaps require joining a club and posting scores from full rounds via Golf NZ. (Save TheGrint app for later!)
Equipment Glossary: Shaft Flex

Shaft flex indicates how much the club bends during the swing. Essential for matching swing speed.

  • 📏 L (Ladies): Very flexible, < 75 mph.
  • 📏 A (Senior): Soft, 75–85 mph.
  • 📏 R (Regular): Medium, 85–95 mph. *Recommended starting point for you (188cm male beginner).*
  • 📏 S (Stiff): Firmer, 95–110 mph.
  • 📏 X (Extra Stiff): Very firm, > 110 mph.
